Should I Have Created Life at All?

I thought I could overcome nature’s boundaries, to create and not just exist. Now I see the pain that creation brings.

What Were the Consequences of My Arrogance?

I acted as if I were a god, but gods do not run from their creations. My arrogance blinded me to what I would unleash.

Do I Owe Anything to My Creation?

It was born because of me, and yet I fled from it. Do I owe it care, guidance, or even love? Or is it doomed because of my neglect?

Did I Let Science Blind Me to Morality?

I let my ambition drown out the question of whether I should create life at all. Science has led me astray.

Who Is the True Monster—Me or the Creature?

Perhaps I am the monster. My neglect created its rage, its vengeance.

Should I Fear My Creation More Than I Fear Myself?

My Creature is powerful, yes, but my own fears, my own mistakes—they have caused far more harm than its strength ever could.

Did I Unleash Chaos on the World?

With a spark of life, I opened the gates to something I could not control. I thought I was creating order, but I only created chaos.

Was the Pursuit of Power Worth the Lives Destroyed?

For every victory, some lives were lost. Would I make the same choice again?
